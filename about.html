About Me

I'm Melvin Morales, a photographer based in Las Vegas, Nevada. My work is rooted in a deep love for the natural landscape — the silence of open desert, the play of light across red rock, the quiet power of wide skies.

Photography, for me, is about slowing down. In a city known for spectacle and speed, I find myself drawn to the opposite — to stillness, to subtlety, to the moments no one stops to notice.

My Practice

I shoot primarily on location throughout the Mojave and Great Basin deserts. Each image is carefully composed to reflect not just what a place looks like, but what it feels like to stand inside it.

My process is slow and deliberate. I return to the same locations across seasons and times of day, building a relationship with the land before I ever press the shutter.
