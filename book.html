Vistas: American West

"Vistas: American West" is a captivating photobook that showcases the rugged beauty and untamed landscapes of the western states of the United States. From the majestic peaks of the Rockies to the vast deserts of the Southwest, each page invites you to immerse yourself in the breathtaking scenery and timeless allure of this iconic region.

Join me on a visual journey through the heart of the American West, where every image tells a story of resilience, exploration, and the enduring spirit of the frontier.

About This Book

This photobook is a separate body of work from the Vistas: Beauty of the Desert exhibit currently on display at the West Charleston Library. While the exhibit focuses exclusively on Nevada, the book captures landscapes from across the broader American West.
