Vistas: Beauty of the Desert

About the Exhibit

Vistas is a photographic exploration of the Mojave and Great Basin deserts — landscapes that most people pass through but rarely stop to witness. This exhibit presents the desert not as an empty place, but as a living, breathing terrain filled with texture, color, and quiet drama.

Each photograph was captured over the course of two years of regular visits to locations across Nevada, Utah, and California. The series invites viewers to see familiar terrain with fresh eyes.

Venue & Hours

The exhibit runs January 17 through August 22 at the West Charleston Library in Las Vegas, Nevada. Admission is free and open to the public during regular library hours.
